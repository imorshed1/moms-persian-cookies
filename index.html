Our Story

A Taste of Persia

Welcome to Shirini Sweets, where the rich culinary traditions of Persia come to life through our handcrafted cookies.

For over 30 years, my mother has been perfecting these traditional recipes, passed down through generations. What started as a passion for sharing our cultural heritage has blossomed into a family business dedicated to bringing authentic Persian flavors to your table.

Each cookie is made with premium ingredients, including aromatic cardamom, delicate rose water, and the finest pistachios. We never use preservatives or artificial flavors, ensuring that every bite is as authentic as it is delicious.

From our family to yours, we invite you to experience the magic of Persian confectionery and the love baked into every cookie.

Our Signature Cookies

Kolompeh

Traditional Persian date-filled cookies from Kerman province, decorated with intricate patterns. These delightful treats feature a buttery dough filled with dates, cardamom, and cinnamon.

Ingredients:

Rice flour Butter Dates Rose water Cardamom

Ghotab

Crispy deep-fried pastries from Yazd, shaped like a half-moon and filled with a mixture of crushed almonds, walnuts, pistachios, and fragrant spices, dusted with powdered sugar.

Ingredients:

Flour Butter Pistachios Almonds Walnuts Cardamom
Order Now

Nan-e Nokhodchi

Delicate melt-in-your-mouth chickpea flour cookies, shaped like a four-leaf clover. These gluten-free treats are lightly scented with cardamom and rose water.

Ingredients:

Chickpea flour Powdered sugar Vegetable oil Cardamom Rose water
Order Now

Shirini Keshmeshi

Soft and chewy raisin cookies that are a popular treat for Persian New Year (Nowruz) celebrations. These cookies are infused with vanilla and studded with plump raisins.

Ingredients:

Flour Butter Raisins Eggs Vanilla
Order Now

Baklava

Layers of delicate phyllo pastry filled with a mixture of chopped nuts and sweetened with honey syrup infused with rose water. Our Persian style baklava is lighter and less sweet than other regional varieties.

Ingredients:

Phyllo dough Pistachios Walnuts Honey Rose water
